SkedogBot Docs

Getting started only takes 3 steps!

  1. Go to http://skedogbot.com and click the "Login with Twitch" button at the top right
  2. Login to your Twitch account, and grant SkedogBot the proper permissions
  3. Click the "Join Channel" button at the top

And that's it! SkedogBot is now in your channel, ready to handle whatever you throw at it! You can now start adding commands, requesting songs, and so much more! Check out the Default Commands page to see what all SkedogBot can do for you!
